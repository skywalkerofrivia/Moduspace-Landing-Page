Common questions.

Honest answers from the team that built the cases.

Why acrylic instead of glass?
Acrylic delivers better optical clarity — no green hue from iron — than glass, without the shatter risk. One bump won't cost you a $300 figure. It's also significantly lighter: easier to assemble and reconfigure.
What's the lead time for orders?
Each Moducase is built to order. Current lead time is 8 months. We confirm your exact timeline when you order. Not happy in the first 30 days? Full cancellation, no questions asked.
Can I expand my display later?
That's the whole point. Every Moducase of the same family shares the same dimensions and connection system. Start with one, add more whenever — the wall grows with your collection.
How much weight can it hold?
Each case safely holds up to 50 lbs, with load-tested stacking up to five units tall. Full specs on every product page.
What if something arrives damaged?
We replace it. No runaround — email us a photo and a replacement ships within 5 days. Every case is insured in transit.
