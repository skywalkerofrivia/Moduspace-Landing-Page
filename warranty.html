      Warranty

      Moducase Display Warranty Overview

      At Moduspace, we understand the importance of quality and reliability when it comes to your display solutions. Our display products are designed and crafted with precision to meet the highest standards. To further enhance your confidence in our products, we offer a comprehensive display warranty.

      Warranty Coverage

      Our display warranty covers:

      • Manufacturing defects
      • Workmanship issues
      • Courier mishandling damages

      It ensures that your Moducase display is free from defects in materials and craftsmanship under normal use and service.

      Exclusions

      The warranty does not cover damages resulting from misuse, accidents, unauthorized modifications, or any alterations made to the display unit. Additionally, normal wear and tear are not covered under this warranty.

      This warranty is voided if the product is used outdoors.

      How Moduspace Will Rectify the Problem

      In the event that you encounter an issue covered by the warranty, please contact our customer support team promptly. Provide them with the necessary details, including:

      • Your proof of purchase. (If you purchased from our official retailers, please provide a copy of your retailer's invoice.)
      • Your email address if you purchased from us directly.
      • A clear description of the issue with pictures.

      Moduspace will examine the provided pictures and decide whether the problem is covered by our warranty. Upon verification of the warranty claim, Moduspace will, at its discretion, replace the defective item.

      Customer Satisfaction

      Moduspace is committed to ensuring customer satisfaction. We value your trust in our products, and our customer support team is dedicated to addressing your concerns promptly and effectively.
