01 — Panels

Crystal clear.
Zero glass risk.

Glass carries a green tint from the iron in its composition — visible the moment you put it next to acrylic. And when it fails, it fails completely. One bump, one knock, and tempered glass shatters without warning, taking your items with it.

Our acrylic panels are optically clearer than glass, shatter-proof, and lighter at every size. Because panels seat directly into the aluminum frame, there's no seam running through the middle of your display — just an uninterrupted view of your collection.

  • 6mm crystal-clear cast acrylic
  • No shatter risk — safe around children and pets
  • Lighter than glass, reducing frame stress
  • Panels are individually replaceable
  • No visible seam — panels sit inside the frame, not in front of it
02 — Frame

No wobble.
No sag. Ever.

A large statue can weigh over 10kg. Stack three cases and you're asking the frame to hold serious weight, case after case, year after year. Budget shelving collapses. Muscle racks buckle. Most display cases aren't engineered for what collectors actually put inside them.

Moducase frames are precision-cut metal alloys — designed to support heavy collectibles and stack securely without flex. Add a case now, add another next year. The system doesn't compromise.

  • Precision-cut metal frame construction
  • Engineered for vertical stacking — no flex over time
  • Uniform dimensions across each series for seamless walls
03 — Sealing

Dust stays out.
You stay in control.

Dust isn't just cosmetic. Fine particles settle into paint detail, joints, and fabric over months. By the time you notice, the grime is embedded — and on a $400 figure, that's damage difficult to undo.

Open shelves guarantee dust. Even closed cases with poor seals let it migrate in slowly. Most DIY attempts end one of two ways: the panel falls because adhesive tape has degraded, or the builder compensates with a single large door spanning multiple cases.

Moducase panels seat on a precision-engineered foot — custom-built — that carries the weight. Magnets seal each panel shut. Access one case. The rest stay closed.

  • Precision edge seals on all acrylic panels
  • Custom panel foot + magnetic closure — no adhesive strips that degrade
  • Significantly reduces dust accumulation between cleanings
  • Panels remove easily for cleaning and rearranging
